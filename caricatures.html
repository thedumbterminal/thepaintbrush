Caricatures

Caricatures have always been a strong point for Michael Terry, drawing them long before becoming an illustrator. Having produced many for big media companies, such as TV Times, Punch, The Times, Sunday Telegraph, Observer Magazine and the Queens Club Tennis Tournament, as well as for well-known individuals themselves, such as Stephen Fry.
