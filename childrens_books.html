Children's Bookss

Children's book illustration is one of Michael Terry's strong points. He loves working on them as it gives him the freedom and imagination free-rein any artist should revel in. A few of his hit works include the likes of 'The Selfish Crocodile,' 'Little Hotchpotch,' 'Rhino's Horns,' 'Sulky Vulture,' 'Captain Wag the Pirate Dog' and a number of Dick King-Smith's books.
